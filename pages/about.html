Chemi's journey is fueled by her deep love for Tibetan heritage, a burning desire to preserve culture and spirituality, and



an unwavering determination to secure a brighter future for her people. She doesn't walk this path alone.



Together with like-minded individuals and organizations, they are breaking barriers, challenging oppression, and striving for a world where everyone can freely express their identity and realize their full potential.

Chemi's heart beats for the freedom and rights of the Tibetan people. Her dedication knows no bounds as she passionately tackles issues that plague her community: political oppression, social and economic discrimination, cultural and religious suppression, and the environmental destruction that threatens their sacred lands.

You might have caught sight of Chemi on various platforms, from local news outlets like CBC and BBC to Teen Vogue and the esteemed OSLO Freedom Forum. Chemi was recognized on Maclean's Power List, where she stood among those forging paths and shaping the very way we think and live. So come and join Chemi. Let's create a world that embraces diversity, protects human rights, and celebrates the beautiful tapestry of our global family. Together, we can make a difference, one small step at a time.
